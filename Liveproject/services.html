Our Services

Accommodation

The sea facing cottage has four spacious rooms with two double beds each, which is ideal for families. The couple's cottage has three rooms with one double bed each. AC and non AC rooms are available at Sea View Tarkarli Resort.The luxury rooms offers most enchanting stay around the coconut trees. We have very open space for children’s to play outdoor games.

Sea View Restaurant

At Sea View Tarkarli Resort, you can enjoy delicious Malvani Veg and non Veg food. Sea food is the speciality of the place. Food is prepared in the authentic Malvani culinary style which is a cross over between Maharashtrian and Goan styles.We also serve home made malvani coastal food.We are specialized in coastal food as well as pure vegetarian food.
