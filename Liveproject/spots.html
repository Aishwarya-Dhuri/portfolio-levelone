Why Visit Tarkarli?

Activities

SCUBA DIVING

The shores of Malvan is amongst the very few places in the country where one could indulge in snorkeling & SCUBA diving. M.T.D.C identified the region near Sindhudurg fort abundant with coral reefs as the primary site for snorkeling and SCUBA Diving.

WATER SPORTS

Tarkarli is now the most sought after destination for water sports in Maharashtra in the entire Konkan stretch. All the water rides like jet skiis, banana & bumper rides are available here including parasailing in the sea. Water sports is organized at Tsunami island.

BOATING

Boating is very popular in Tarkarli. There are many sightseeing options like the Nivati fort, Bhogwe beach etc which are visited by boat. There also are some exotic island like Burnt island off the coast of Malvan. Boating in the Karli backwaters is also very popular..

Malvani Cuisine Food

If you are a food lover then you should definately Visit Tarkarli to get the rel taste of delicious Malvani Non-veg food.

Sightseeing in Tarkarli

STAR ATTRACTIONS

Sindhudurga Fort

Built between 1664-67 AD by Shivaji Maharaj, the Sindhudurg fort has it foundation laid on molten lead. One of the best preserved sea forts it has a four kms long rampart with 42 bastions. The fort is approachable from the Malvan pier by boat.

Beaches

Tarkarli is a long stretch of beach which extends from Devbaug beach to its south to Malvan jetty to its north across Wayiri beach. Due to the popularity of Tarkarli beach all these beaches are also considered as Tarkarli beach by the tourists!

Rock Garden

Just Behind the Malvan Police Station, half Kilometer away from Malvan jetty near the Arase Mahal is the Malvan Rock Gardens close to the beach. Malvan Rock Gardens is a beautifuly manicured garden located near the Chivla beach.

Tsunami Island

Located off the coast of Devbaug half a kilometer from the resort, Tsunami island is the hub of all the water sports activities in Tarkarli. Devbaug sangam where Karli backwaters meets the sea is one of the most picturesque spots in Malvan.

OTHER ATTRACTIONS

JAY GANESH TEMPLE

Located in Medha locality of Malvan near the rock gardens is the Jay Ganesh Temple. The temple is famous for its pure gold idol of the diety.

RAMESHWAR TEMPLE

Achara boasts the historical temple of Lord Shiva, popularly known as ‘inamdar Shri Dev Rameshwar’ Architecture of the temple is grand and spectacular.

KARLI BACKWATERS

The Karli backwaters run parallel to the beach with Devbaug wedged between the two. A evening boat ride up the backwaters can be a relaxing and joyful experience.

ISLANDS NEAR TARKARLI

There are some exotic islands off the coast from Malvan. The Nivati rocks is one of the beautiful rocky island here apart from the lighthouse island and burnt island.

LADY OF ROSARY CHURCH

Our Lady Of Rosary Church is a beautiful church very similar to Goan churches in Malvan located on the way to the Malvan rock gardens from the town.

One has the highest chance of spotting a dolphins during a dolphin watch boat ride amongst the beaches in the entire stretch of Konkan while boating near the shores of Tarkarli. Early mornings is the best time for a dolphin watch boat ride. Even during doing the parasailing in the mornings there is a good chance of spotting these friendly mammals.
