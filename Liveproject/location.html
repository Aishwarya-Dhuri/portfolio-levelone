How to Reach

By Air

Dabolim airport about 100 km away is located in the neighbouring state of goa.

By Rail

Nearest railhead is Kudal, 35 km. via Nerurpar on Konkan Railway, Kankavali Railhead is also nearby.

By Road

Mumbai - Tarkarli , 546 km.
Pune - Tarkarli , 410 km.
Kolhapur - Tarkarli , 160 km.
Ganpatipule - Tarkarli , 135 km.
Malvan - Tarkarli , 7 km.
State Transport Buses available from Mumbai, Kolhapur, Pune to Malvan and from Malvan to Tarkarli. Rickshaws are also avaliable from Malvan.
